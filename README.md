# Insurance Analytics - 

![Insurance Power BI Dashboard](screenshots/Insurance%20Project%20Power%20BI%20Dashboard%20pic.png)

## 🎯 Project Objective

This **Insurance Analytics Project** provides a comprehensive **Weekly Branch Performance Dashboard** for an Insurance company. The analysis focuses on tracking sales performance across three major income classes — **Cross Sell, New Business, and Renewal** — while monitoring key activities like meetings, invoices, opportunities, and achievement against targets.

**Key Business Goals:**
- Track achievement vs budget for Cross Sell, New & Renewal
- Monitor Account Executive performance
- Analyze Opportunity pipeline and stage-wise revenue
- Support data-driven decisions for branch performance improvement

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Microsoft Excel** – Pivot Tables, Charts & Static Dashboard
- **Power BI** – Interactive Dashboard with Slicers & KPIs
- **Tableau** – Advanced Visualizations & Dashboard Design
- **SQL** – Data Analysis & Business Queries

---

## 📊 Key Metrics & KPIs

- **Cross Sell, New & Renewal Performance** (Achieved vs Budget vs Invoice)
- **Achievement %** (Placed & Invoiced)
- **Number of Meetings** by Account Executive
- **Number of Invoices** by Account Executive
- **Opportunity Stage Funnel** by Revenue
- **Top Opportunities** by Revenue
- **Product-wise Opportunity Distribution**

## 📸 Dashboard Views

### 1. Excel Dashboard
Static Weekly Branch Dashboard featuring:
- Cross Sell, New & Renewal performance bars (Invoice, Achieved, Budget)
- Achievement % for each category
- Yearly Meeting Count
- No. of Meetings & Invoices by Account Executive
- Opportunity Product Distribution (Pie Chart)
- Stage Funnel by Revenue
- Top Open Opportunities

### 2. Power BI Dashboard
Interactive dashboard with:
- Renewal, New & Cross Sell cards
- Achievement percentages
- No. of Invoices by Account Executive (stacked bar)
- Top 5 Opportunities by Revenue
- Opportunity Product Distribution
- Stage Funnel by Revenue
- Meetings by Account Executive

### 3. Tableau Dashboard
Clean and structured visualizations including:
- Achievement % across all categories
- No. of Meetings & Invoices by Account Executive
- Opportunity by Revenue (Top 4)
- Stage Funnel by Revenue
- Opportunity Product Distribution
- Open Opportunities

## 💾 SQL Queries Included
